How we use your data

We don't.

No account, no tracking, no analytics, no ads. Your favorites and settings stay in your browser, on your device.

The one honest caveat: to fetch the forecast, your location is sent to the National Weather Service, and when you search a place it's sent to OpenStreetMap. They need it to do their job — neither tracks you, and we never see it.

Weather data comes from the NWS, provided as-is — don't rely on it for life-safety decisions.
